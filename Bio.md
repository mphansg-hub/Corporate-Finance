Mai Phan is a senior corporate executive at PNJ Group (HOSE: PNJ), serving as Chief of Staff to the Board of Directors and Head of Investor Relations. With over 15 years of leadership in governance, capital markets, and enterprise strategy at one of Vietnam's largest listed corporations, she brings direct practitioner experience to corporate finance study.
Her expertise spans corporate governance, investor relations, and securities compliance, grounded in Vietnam's Enterprise Law (No. 59/2020/QH14) and Securities Law (No. 54/2019/QH14). Key achievements include a 25% increase in institutional investor engagement, 15% annual cost reduction, and a Board governance restructure that measurably improved executive decision quality.
Mai holds dual Bachelor's degrees from Northwood University and Hotel Institute Montreux, and is VIOD-certified in Corporate Governance and Corporate Secretaryship. She is currently pursuing her Vietnam Executive MBA at the University of Hawaiʻi (expected 2027), focusing on financial analysis and capital allocation strategy.
