## Background

Founded in 1929 and listed on HKEx in December 2011, Chow Tai Fook operates approximately 7,500 points of sale across Mainland China, Hong Kong, Macau, and international markets. In FY2024 (year ended 31 March 2024), the Group reported revenue of HK$108,713 million (+14.8% YoY) and core operating profit of HK$12,163 million (+28.9% YoY), with a core operating profit margin of 11.2%. This compares to PNJ Group's FY2023 revenue of approximately USD 900 million — a roughly 15× scale differential in the same product category: gold, platinum, diamond, and gem-set jewellery.

The analytical scope covers FY2023 (year ended 31 March 2023) and FY2024 (year ended 31 March 2024), providing two years of IFRS-audited comparable data sufficient for trend analysis across all six ratio categories in the course template.
